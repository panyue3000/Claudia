Subject: Updated heart age manuscript and MWCCS internal review submission

Dear all,

Thank you again for your thoughtful comments and suggestions on the heart age manuscript. I have revised the AIDS submission version to address the major points raised across the co-author reviews.

The key updates include softening the interpretation of the CD4 finding, removing speculative language from the abstract and Results, clarifying that the cross-sectional analysis is intended to establish the baseline distribution of PREVENT-derived heart age before future longitudinal work, and de-emphasizing the PTSD findings because PCL-C data were available for only a small subset of participants. I also clarified the socioeconomic covariate rationale. We tested insurance adjustment in a sensitivity analysis, and the NHB race/ethnicity coefficient was not materially attenuated; because this was intended as an internal robustness check rather than a primary model, I did not add another formal table to the manuscript. The limitations section was also streamlined, and the MWCCS acknowledgments were updated using the current recommended language.

I am submitting the revised manuscript to the MWCCS internal review committee at the same time. Once it is approved by MWCCS, I will submit it to AIDS and keep everyone posted on the submission and review process.

Best,
Yue
